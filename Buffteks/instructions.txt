Buffteks

STUDENT		TEAM
PROJECT		CLIENT

Team of students that get together to work on projects that create websites for clients


Create first draft of entities


Business Rules:
STUDENTS can be on 1 or many TEAMS
TEAMS can have 1 or many STUDENTS


CLIENT can have 1 or many PROJECTS
PROJECTS can have only 1 CLIENT

TEAMS can have 1 or many PROJECTS
PROJECTS can have only one TEAM


Database Name: Buffteks
Table Names:
    STUDENT
        Attributes:
            StudentId - PK
            FirstName
            LastName
            Email
    STUDENT_TEAM
        Attributes:
            StudentId - PK/FK
            TeamId - PK/FK
    TEAM
        Attributes:
            TeamId - PK
            Name
            ProjectId - FK
    CLIENT
        Attributes:
            ClientId - PK
            ClientContactName
            CompanyName
            ClientContactEmail
    PROJECT
        Attributes:
            ProjectId - PK
            ProjectName
            ClientId - FK
